Bridging the Gap: Early Childhood Education in Lagos

By Chinaza Onuoha • September 30, 2025

The problem: low investment and uneven outcomes

Early childhood education (ECE) in Lagos faces underinvestment, inconsistent teacher training, and infrastructure gaps that limit learning outcomes for the youngest learners. These structural issues compound across neighborhoods and socio-economic groups.

A layered solution

A combination of policy advocacy, community partnerships, and low-cost tech-enabled teacher supports can raise classroom quality at scale. Below are key components:

  • Targeted teacher coaching and micro-credentials.
  • Simple, low-bandwidth digital resources for lesson planning.
  • Community-led monitoring & transparent funding channels.

Evidence & pilot data

Small pilot programs show measurable gains in engagement and foundational literacy after 6 months. Scaling requires coordinated funding and continuous evaluation.
